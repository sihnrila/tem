## Image Direction Guide

### ARCHIVE STUDIO
- Keywords: bright studio fashion, minimal beige clothing, soft daylight editorial, clean tailoring
- Mood: calm, structured, airy
- Lighting: diffuse daylight, soft shadows
- Background: warm white / light beige studio
- Ratio: 4:5
- Color grading: warm neutral, slight desaturation, soft contrast
- Editorial campaign: model in beige coat near white studio wall with soft window light
- PDP image guide: 1 hero (4:5), 3 close-ups (fabric, seam, button), 1 full body

### SILENT FORM
- Keywords: muted neutral fashion, gray minimal editorial, tonal layering, quiet silhouette
- Mood: introspective, weighted, calm
- Lighting: lower contrast, soft side light
- Background: pale gray / taupe, matte texture
- Ratio: 4:5
- Color grading: cool-neutral, low saturation, gentle shadow depth
- Editorial campaign: model in layered neutrals with shadowed wall, slow movement
- PDP image guide: 1 hero (4:5), 2 detail shots, 1 tonal flat lay, 1 back view

### MONO HOUSE
- Keywords: black and white fashion, graphic minimal editorial, high contrast styling, typography fashion
- Mood: precise, graphic, bold
- Lighting: crisp contrast, clear edges
- Background: white cyclorama, sharp black props
- Ratio: 4:5
- Color grading: monochrome lean, strong contrast, clean whites
- Editorial campaign: model in black coat against white wall with bold shadow line
- PDP image guide: 1 hero (4:5), 1 silhouette shot, 2 detail shots, 1 contrast close-up
